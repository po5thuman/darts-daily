About DartsDaily

Darts Daily is your daily hub for everything professional darts. Built by a darts fan, for darts fans.

We cover the full PDC calendar — from the Premier League and European Tour to the World Championship — bringing you the latest news, tournament dates, player profiles, daily stats and trivia all in one place.

Whether you're looking for upcoming events near you, want to brush up on your darts knowledge or simply stay up to date with the latest from the oche — Darts Daily has you covered, every day.
