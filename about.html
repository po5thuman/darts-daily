About Darts Daily

Your daily hub for everything professional darts. Built by a darts fan, for darts fans.

Our Story

Darts Daily was born from a simple idea: darts fans deserve a dedicated daily destination that brings together everything they love about the sport in one place.

As lifelong darts fans, we found ourselves constantly jumping between different sites, social media accounts and apps just to stay up to date. Player stats were on one site, tournament schedules on another, news scattered across social media, and equipment reviews buried in forums. There was no single place that had it all.

So we built one.

Darts Daily launched in 2025 with a mission to be the most useful, most comprehensive and most enjoyable darts website on the internet. Every day, we publish fresh content including player profiles, trivia questions, stats, historical moments and news — all curated for darts fans who want their daily fix.

Editorial Standards

Everything we publish is:

  • Accurate — we fact-check all stats, records and information against official PDC sources
  • Original — all guides, reviews and content are written by our team, never copied
  • Unbiased — our product reviews and recommendations are based on genuine testing and research
  • Up to date — we update our guides and content regularly to keep everything current
  • Honest — we clearly label all affiliate links and sponsored content
