💡 Did You Know?
Wright's wife Joanne designs many of his outfits and hairstyles. He once arrived at a tournament with his hair styled as the Scottish flag.

Peter Wright is one of the most colourful characters in darts — literally. The two-time World Champion is as famous for his wild hair and outfits as he is for his dart throwing, but behind the showmanship is one of the most complete players on tour.

Daily Question
One question. Do you know your darts?

How many times has Peter Wright won the PDC World Championship?

Peter Wright has won the PDC World Championship twice — in 2020 defeating Michael van Gerwen in the final, and in 2022 defeating Michael Smith. Remarkably he did not win his first world title until he was 49 years old.
