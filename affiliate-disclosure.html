Affiliate Disclosure

Transparency is important to us. Here's how affiliate links work on Darts Daily and how they support our free content.

📅 Last updated: April 2026

What Are Affiliate Links?

Darts Daily is a participant in affiliate programmes, including the Amazon Associates Programme. This means that some links on our site are "affiliate links".

When you click on an affiliate link and make a purchase, we may earn a small commission from the retailer. This comes at absolutely no extra cost to you — you pay the same price whether you use our link or go directly to the retailer.

💡
Key Point
Using our affiliate links costs you nothing extra. The price you pay is exactly the same. The retailer pays us a small referral fee from their own margin.

Why We Use Affiliate Links

Running Darts Daily takes time, effort and money. Affiliate commissions help us:

  • Keep the site free — all our content is free to access, always
  • Cover running costs — hosting, domain, tools and services
  • Invest in content — creating more guides, reviews and resources
  • Stay independent — we're not owned by any darts brand or retailer

Our Promise to You

We take our recommendations seriously. Here's what we guarantee:

✅

Honest Recommendations

We only recommend products we genuinely believe are good value and relevant to darts fans. We will never recommend a bad product just because it has a higher commission.

✅

Unbiased Reviews

Our editorial opinions are never influenced by affiliate partnerships. If a product isn't good, we'll say so — even if we could earn a commission from it.

✅

Full Transparency

Every page that contains affiliate links clearly states so. You'll always see a note like "Affiliate links — we earn a small commission at no extra cost to you."

✅

No Pressure

You are never obligated to click our affiliate links. Our content is valuable whether you buy anything or not. The links are simply there if you find them helpful.

On Darts Daily, affiliate links are identified by:

  • A note at the bottom of product sections: "Affiliate links — we earn a small commission at no extra cost to you."
